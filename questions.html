Frequently Asked Questions

  • since years practicing practice drawing learning time

    How long have you been drawing?

    "The answer is nothing new or unique. I draw since I was very little, took up on digital mediums at teenage years and make an effort to practice every day."

  • mentors learn education school classes lessons courses college university

    Did you take any courses or go to art school?

    "None, if anything I attended a public vocational high school where I coursed Architecture Drawings. It only covered the very basics of architecture but it turned very fruitful in my efforts to immerse characters in their worlds. Other than that, everything I know is thanks to the internet."

  • tools tablet computer programs software hardware materials

    What tools do you use for digital art?

    Hardware: iMac with a Wacom Bamboo tablet, iPad Pro with an Apple Pencil
    Software: Clip Art Studio, Photoshop and Procreate.

  • mediums traditional watercolor pencils styles

    Do you draw on other mediums?

    "I'll experiment with anything I can get my hands on: watercolor, acrylics, color pencils, oils, markers, ink, on paper, on canvas, on concrete...does latte counts?"

  • order commissions buy sell open announce

    How can I commission you?

    "I open slots through my deviantArt or Tumblr. My latest pattern is opening every 2-3 months, I try to announce openings a week or two prior."

  • conventions exhibit table tabling comic con

    Do you exhibit in conventions?

    "Every two years I exhibit at Puerto Rico Comic Con."

  • strive goal dream art career professionalism

    What do you hope to accomplish in your artistic career?

    "I strive to make the kind of artwork that reconnects the viewer with their inner child; to stimulate the creativity of the viewer with flamboyant colors, characters and settings; and to exhort appreciation to the beauty of our planet.
